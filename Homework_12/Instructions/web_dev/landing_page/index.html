    Cancer Research

    This is a fully functional website, created by Ly-Bach Truong, showcasing his recent analysis of pharmaceutical data that utilizes several Python libraries, such as pandas, NumPy, and matplotlib.pyplot.

    In his analysis, he breaks down the relationships between the tumor response, metastatic response, and survival rates of several mice groups separated by drug usage and plots his findings accordingly. This portfolio item was developed as an assignment at UCI's Data Analytics Bootcamp.
